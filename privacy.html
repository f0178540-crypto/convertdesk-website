Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ConvertDesk is a desktop file conversion application developed by AppLab.

Data collection

ConvertDesk does not require users to create an account for basic local use. Files selected for conversion are processed by the application according to the selected conversion workflow.

Payments

If ConvertDesk Pro purchases are enabled, payment processing may be handled by a third-party payment provider. Payment details are processed by the payment provider and are not stored directly by AppLab.

Support

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and any information you include in your message.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@convertdesk.com
