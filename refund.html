Refund Policy

Refund Policy for ConvertDesk.

This Refund Policy explains the planned refund approach for ConvertDesk Pro purchases. Payments are not live yet, so this policy will be finalized before public paid release.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Current payment status

ConvertDesk Pro payments are not currently live. The public checkout, license activation and payment processor integration will be added only after final release preparation.

2. Policy status

This Refund Policy is prepared for the future ConvertDesk Pro launch. The final version may be updated when the payment processor, licensing system, pricing and product delivery method are selected.

If a third-party payment processor is used, refund handling may also be subject to that provider's checkout, billing and refund rules.

3. General refund approach

The intended refund approach is to review refund requests fairly, especially when there is a verified technical issue that prevents normal use of ConvertDesk Pro and support cannot reasonably resolve it.

Users should contact support first when they experience installation, activation or conversion issues.

4. Possible refund reasons

Duplicate purchase

A duplicate accidental purchase may be eligible for review.

Failed activation

If a valid license cannot be activated and support cannot resolve the issue, a refund may be reviewed.

Technical issue

A verified technical issue that prevents normal use may be eligible for review.

Wrong product purchase

Accidental wrong purchases may be reviewed depending on the payment processor rules.

5. Non-refundable cases

Some situations may not qualify for a refund, depending on the final payment processor policy and product licensing terms.

Unsupported expectations

Refunds may not be available if the product works as described but does not match an unsupported expectation.

Failure to keep backups

Users are responsible for keeping backups of important files before conversion.

Misuse

Refunds may not be available in cases of misuse, abuse, fraud or violation of the Terms of Use.

Third-party issues

Issues caused by unsupported systems, corrupted files or third-party software may require technical review.

6. Support before refund

If ConvertDesk Pro becomes available for purchase, users should contact support before requesting a refund. Support may ask for technical details to understand and reproduce the issue.

Windows version

Example: Windows 10 64-bit or Windows 11 64-bit.

ConvertDesk version

Example: ConvertDesk 1.0.0.

Purchase details

Order ID, checkout email or license key may be required after payments go live.

Issue details

Include error messages, affected formats and steps to reproduce the problem.

7. Payment processor rules

ConvertDesk Pro may use a payment processor such as Paddle, FastSpring, PayPro Global or another selected provider.

The selected provider may handle checkout, taxes, invoices, subscriptions, order records, chargebacks and refunds. Final refund handling may depend on that provider's policies.

8. Subscriptions and lifetime licenses

Final ConvertDesk Pro pricing has not been launched yet. ConvertDesk Pro may be offered as a lifetime license, subscription or another pricing model.

Lifetime license

If a lifetime license is offered, refund terms will be clearly published before checkout.

Subscription

If a subscription is offered, renewal, cancellation and refund rules will be published before checkout.

9. Chargebacks and fraud

Fraudulent payments, chargeback abuse, license misuse or violation of the Terms of Use may result in license suspension or restriction of access to paid features.

Users should contact support first if there is a billing problem.

10. Changes to this Refund Policy

This Refund Policy may be updated before or after public launch to reflect the final payment processor, pricing, license model, product delivery method or legal requirements.

The latest version will be available on the ConvertDesk website.
